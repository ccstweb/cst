Applications

CST specializes in the development of semiconductor wafer fabrication plants, assembly, and testing industries. Slurry is a mixture of abrasive particles and chemical additives, with the main components of the abrasive particles being alumina, silica, and ceria. The chemical additives are tailored to meet user requirements. The key to achieving high-quality grinding lies in the slurry, and we provide solutions for various Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) processes. Our systems are equipped with high precision and fast supply technologies, making them suitable for both existing and new process development. We can also customize the SDS design to meet specific slurry requirements or offer tailored systems based on customers’ needs.

About CST

CST’s Journey

　　CST specialize in developing low shear stress SDS system and higher wafer production yield to semiconductor manufacturing industry. Slurry is a mixture of abrasive particles and chemical additives, with the main components of the abrasive particles being alumina, silica, and ceria. The chemical additives are tailored to meet user requirements. The key to achieving high-quality grinding lies in the slurry, and we provide solutions for various Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) processes. Our systems are equipped with high precision and fast supply technologies, making them suitable for both existing and new process development. We can also develop customized the SDS design to meet specific slurry requirements or offer tailored systems based on customers’ needs.
